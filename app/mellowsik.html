Introduction

Instrumental music is defined as music intended to be performed by a musical instrument or group of instruments. Scientific research tells us that learning to play an instrument is good for your brain, so perhaps it shouldn't be surprising a new study published in Evolutionary Behavioral Sciences suggests that those who prefer instrumental music tend to be more intelligent.
